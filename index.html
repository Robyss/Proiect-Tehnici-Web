About Us

What is SnowRiders?

SnowRiders is a platform for snowboarding enthusiasts. Here you will find guides and tutorials to ski in a more thrilling and correct way, but also reach out to dedicated snowboarding equipment that we consider the most suitable for this spectacular sport.

Why us?

Our team includes people with decades of experience who spend every winter in the mountains. We have created this platform to bring all the advice of the most expert riders in one place. We also have the highest quality equipment for a wonderful snowboarding experience.
